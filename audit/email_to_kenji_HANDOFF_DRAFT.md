Subject: RE: CG MCIPAC Endorsement Request

Kenji,

Quick status update from our side and one deliverable for your read.

Coming out of the working session last Friday and your follow-up
note from 1 May, we put together a single dashboard that pulls
the 3d Medical Battalion consolidation at Camp Kinser into one
place. The intent is for this to be our project charter going
forward, so we can stop circulating multiple attachments and
instead point at one document that covers the same ground.

The dashboard aggregates everything from the G-5 package into
six tabs: command intent and operational driver, how the action
evolved (Tab A through the CG signed letter), current state
including the component-level status of which elements have
moved to Kinser and which are still in transition, the work and
the team, the six-building Kinser footprint (107, 300, 613, 400,
1225, 508) with the Foster turnover picture, and the plans we
need to reconcile against (UDP3, HAP, OKICON, Kinser Towers,
ICG) along with the risks, the way forward, and the open
questions we still owe a back-brief on.

The whole thing is editable in the browser. There is an Edit Mode
toggle at the top right that opens every field for editing,
including the logos (click to replace). When you are happy with
the changes, Save HTML downloads the current state as a single
file. No special tools needed.

A couple of items still open that you should know about. There
is a real personnel-count disparity on Bravo Surg Co between
Tab A (180 BICs), Tab C (181), and the strategic basing action
(185). It is logged inline and on the Open Questions card; I am
holding it as TBD until we can validate against the authoritative
T/O. Building 300 has been walked and has Life Safety Health
concerns plus extensive roof work needed, so the final medical
footprint is going to depend on how 300 scopes out. Engineer
assignment on our side is still being determined.

In parallel we have started pulling in the BFRs from 3d MLG,
sorting through the relevant ones, and beginning to flush out
where they line up cleanly against the Kinser footprint and where
they need refinement. Once we have a working set we will walk it
against the iNFADS pull, or against the building walk itself if
iNFADS is not current, and feed the deltas back into the
dashboard.

Please take a pass at the dashboard when you have a chance.
Anything you want changed, deleted, reframed, or added, edit it
directly and send the file back, or flag it and we will update on
our end. The point is for this to stay useful to you and to the
back-briefs going up the chain, so your edits are how we keep it
accurate.

Happy to set up another working session whenever fits your
schedule, or to just trade comments in the file. Whatever works
on your end works on ours.
