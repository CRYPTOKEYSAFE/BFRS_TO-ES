Newly surfaced finding (held for next session)
================================================================

Finding: H&S Co (M28261) has 198 TE rows in the workbook, all
inherited from the 2017 NAVFAC MIDLANT template baseline. None of them
have a TFSMS source. Per Apex Omega rules 1, 3, and 4, the entire H&S
T/E side is suspect, not just R507. The 197 inherited rows have
real-looking quantities (e.g., R2 PISTOL,9MM qty 111; R5 INSERTS,SAPI
qty 610; R10 COT,FOLDING qty 168) which descend from a 2nd Med Bn
template, not from any 3d Med Bn primary source.

Held for next session under ruflo orchestration: re-source the entire
H&S Co T/E side from an authoritative 3d Med Bn document (Master TO&E
extract, MERIT pull, MCBUL 3000 series, or user-supplied list) and
either confirm or replace the 197 inherited quantities. Apex Omega
rule 4 applies row-by-row.

Lesson learned about agent prompt design: the first parallel research
agent this session (Find H&S equipment source) reported "no M28261
rows in TE" and "Row 507 is not an H&S row" because it confused the
CCN column with TAMCN and missed the UIC column entirely. Its negative
findings on the workbook were false. Verification by direct openpyxl
read found 198 H&S rows. Future parallel agents that report ABSENT on
workbook data must include a column-letter audit (header row dump
plus a sample of cells from each named column) before the ABSENT
claim is accepted.
