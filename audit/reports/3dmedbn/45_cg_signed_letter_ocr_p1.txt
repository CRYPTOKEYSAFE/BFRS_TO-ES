1. Purpose. 3d Medical Battalion (3d Med Bn) is disaggregated throughout Camp Foster and Camp Kinser
with various elements that are out of position to provide cohesive and timely support to III Marine
Expeditionary Force (III MEF). In tangent to the relocation of Bravo, Surgical Company, reference (b), 3d
Med Bn seeks to consolidate its elements on Camp Kinser. Ill MEF requires eight Surgical Platoons to
meet wartime requirements but is only staffed to three within the First Island Chain (FIC). II] MEF must
consolidate its Role 2 structure on Okinawa where it will be most effective and able to support mission
requirements,

2. Background. Relocation of 3d Med Bn to Camp Kinser enables the battalion to co-locate Alpha Surgical
Company, Bravo Surgical Company, Headquarters and Services Company, including the Motor
Transportation and Utilities (MT/UT) section and the Medical Skills Training Center (MSTC). Due to the
limited adequate space on Camp Foster, 3d Med Bn has already relocated the MT/UT section and MSTC to
Camp Kinser. Under efforts to maximize space on Camp Foster, the MT/UT section was reallocated under
Combat Logistics Regiment 3. While this allocation allowed for maximization of Camp Foster motor pool
space, it did not facilitate tactical medical operations. The MSTC, which provides basic to advanced
medical training and skills currency to all of III MEF, was in a warehouse space that did not provide
adequate equipment storage or an adequate training area to simulate medical operations in a tactical field
environment year-round.

3. Impacts. 3d Marine Logistics Group (3d MLG) conducted an internal assessment that identified a way
ahead in which 3d Med Bn, in its entirety, can relocate to Camp Kinser. The current tenants of each of the
impacted facilities are 3d MLG units. Consolidation into the listed facilities maximizes 3d MLG’s use of its
allocated buildings while increasing unit integrity. Furthermore, basing 3d Med Bn on Camp Kinser
improves the operational posture as Medical Logistics Company, Combat Logistics Regiment 35
(MEDLOG, CLR-35) is already on Camp Kinser. This proximity vastly improves response time, as
MEDLOG houses all the deployable medical equipment used by 3d Med Bn.

4. Request. Il MEF, 3d MLG formally requests current Basing Actions aboard Marine Corps Base Butler
(MCBB), Camp Kinser, Okinawa Japan, for 3d Medical Battalion. __
