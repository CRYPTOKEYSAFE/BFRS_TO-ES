================================================================
What these are: current iNFADS facility supply at named Kinser buildings
================================================================

Bldg 107, 300, 400, 508, 613 are the buildings named in the CG MCIPAC
endorsement (April 2026 draft, in repo) as the proposed 3d MED BN
landing spots. Bldg 350 is the adjacent Army HAP compound named in
the basing-assessment dashboard timeline.

These are NOT enclosure (2) "Proposed Facilities Layout" of the CG
signed letter (2 Feb 2026). They are room-by-room schedules of
CURRENT occupancy and CURRENT CCN tagging at the named buildings.
The 3d MED BN UICs (M13020, M28261, M28262, M28263) do NOT appear
in any of these schedules. The M67400 entries that do appear refer
to "H&S BN MCIPAC MCB CP BUTLER JA" (the installation's H&S
battalion under MCIPAC), which shares the M67400 UIC and is a
different unit from 3d MED BN.

Use these as facility-supply data. Do not treat them as proposed
3d MED BN demand.

================================================================
Application
================================================================

Per CG MCIPAC endorsement (April 2026 draft), proposed 3d MED BN
allotment names: Bldg 107 BN HQ co-use, Bldg 300 MSTC, Bldg 400
consolidated armory bay, Bldg 508 supply, Bldg 613 MT/UT, plus
Bldg 1225 housing and the medical-specific buildings TBD. Note
Bldg 1225 schedule was not supplied in this batch. The medical
clinical facility (Series 500 53010 Dispensary candidate per
audit/reports/3dmedbn/44_gsf_findings.txt Defect 1) is also
absent from the supplied schedules.

These schedules support a facility-supply sanity check once the
proposed BFR demand by CCN is computed. They do not, in their own
form, supply enclosure (2) "Proposed Facilities Layout" demand
data. The BFR demand calculation must come from the T/O&E driven
side of the pipeline, with these schedules used only as a
matched-against-supply cross-reference.
