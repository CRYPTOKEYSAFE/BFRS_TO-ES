Critical schema finding
================================================================

The 2031 Master TO&E TE_3 sheet has 27 columns including the
exact columns the 3d MED BN BFR's CCN sheets reference but the
BFR's own TE leaves blank:

  col 17  L, Ft
  col 18  W, Ft
  col 19  H, Ft
  col 20  Volume EA, ft3
  col 21  Volume Total, ft3
  col 22  Space Factor              <- CCN 21451 needs this
  col 23  Type Code
  col 24  NSY                       <- CCN 14312 needs this
  col 25  NTG Factor                <- CCN 14312 needs this
  col 26  Cost Code
  col 27  Primary/Component

The 3d MED BN BFR was built from a 2nd MED BN / 1st MED BN
Camp Pendleton template (now confirmed by externalLink3 in the
purged ghost links). That template's CCN sheets reference Space
Factor, NSY, and NTG via VLOOKUP into the BFR's own TE. The
BFR's TE never populated those columns; the data was always in
the 2031 Master TO&E and never imported.

Net effect: every CCN sheet whose math depends on Space Factor /
NSY / NTG returns 0 for the equipment-driven side, dragging the
TOTAL REQUIREMENT cell down to either 0 or a constant offset.
